en-dash &ndash; – Used to mark ranges between two values 15–20
em-dash &mdash; — Used to mark pauses between words This sentence has a pause—because of this em-dash.
hyphen - - Used to connect words or add a line break mid-word This page is about typography-oriented principles.
typographer’s quotes &ldquo; &rdquo; “...” Quotation marks around something someone has said “This is a quotation from a source.”
apostrophe &lsquo; &rsquo; ‘...’ Used for contractions and sub-quotes “This quotation has a ‘sub-quotation’ and it’s also got a contraction.”
non-breaking space &nbsp;   Used to avoid misplaced line breaks This has a non-breaking space at the end to avoid runt words.
wordbreak <wbr> Tells the browser where to add line breaks if needed Fernstraßenbauprivatfinanzierungsgesetz will break in multiple places as needed, without hyphens.
shy hyphen <shy> - Tells the browser where to hyphenate words when breaking across lines Fernstraßen­bau­privat­finanzierungs­gesetz will break in multiple places as needed, with hyphens.
